Agriculture Tools

Tractor

Agricultural vehicle used for plowing, harvesting, and other farming activities.

Combine Harvester

Agricultural machine used for harvesting grain crops such as wheat, corn, and soybeans.

Plow

Agricultural tool used for turning over soil in preparation for planting.

Cultivator

Agricultural tool used for tilling soil and removing weeds between rows of crops.

Seeder

Agricultural machine used for planting seeds in rows or on a broadcast basis.

Irrigation

Irrigation is the artificial application of water to the soil for the purpose of agricultural crop production
